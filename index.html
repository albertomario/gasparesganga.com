About

My journey as a computer programmer started in 1998 with Visual Basic 6, which remained my favourite developing tool for many years before I specialized in Databases, GIS/Spatial technologies and Web programming.

Being IT supervisor, GIS analyst & lead developer at Setin has given me the opportunity and time to master Web Mapping related technologies, including PostgreSQL, PostGIS, MapServer, PROJ.4, GDAL but also PHP, Javascript, jQuery, HTML and CSS, taking care of all the analysis/design/development process.

I occasionally give courses and seminars about GIS & Web Mapping and I'm keen to work on other projects when I have time.
